## Bot Behaviour

- The Bot monitors comments on subreddits where it is installed for the pattern `[[script name]]`.
- When a match is found, the Bot queries the public API at [botcscripts.com](https://botcscripts.com) and posts a reply with a link to the matching script.
- The Bot does not moderate, remove, or report content.
- The Bot acts only within the subreddits where it has been installed by a moderator.
